The SharedWork Ohio program helps prevent layoffs by allowing employers to uniformly reduce affected employees hours by 10 to 50 percent while permitting the employees to receive a prorated unemployment. The Shared Work program is designed to help employers manage business cycles and seasonal adjustments while helping to spare their workers the hardships of full unemployment.
